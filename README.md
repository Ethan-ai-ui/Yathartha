## Yathartha — Truth in the Age of Information

Yathartha (यथार्थ) is an AI-powered news and information verification platform designed to help people in Nepal identify misinformation, fake news, and misleading narratives in real time. In a digital environment where rumors, political propaganda, and manipulated content spread faster than facts, Yathartha empowers citizens with clarity, context, and truth.

Our goal is simple: make verified information accessible, understandable, and trustworthy for everyone.
